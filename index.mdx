## What Presidio does

Presidio helps you find and de-identify PII in your data pipelines. The core workflow is:

1. **Analyze** — detect PII entities (names, emails, SSNs, phone numbers, and more) in text
2. **Anonymize** — replace, redact, mask, hash, or encrypt detected PII
3. **Redact** — remove PII from images using OCR-based detection
